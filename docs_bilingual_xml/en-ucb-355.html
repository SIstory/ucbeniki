NotesAleksander Rožek is the author of the German original. The present work is a translation of the fourth edition of Rožko's work Wörterbuch zu Hoffmanns Historia antiqua und Caesar de bello gallico, which, according to Hriberšek, was prepared by a group of 'five high school professors' (Hriberšek, 2005, 151).
