NotesCOBISS also lists an edition from the UKM dated 'before 1859'. This is certainly true, but we can certainly place the work a little further back in time. As Škafar points out, Alojys Weitzinger died on September 25, 1844. Given that the present copy still mentions Aloys, but not his son and successor Johann Albert, we can definitely place the issue before September 25, 1844. The work was revised in 1864 by Vendelin Ratkovič and Jožef Borovnjak (Škafar, 1978, 30 and Novak, 2013 and Krajnc Vrečko, 2013 and Šlebinger, 2013).
