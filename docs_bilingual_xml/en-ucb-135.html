NotesThe first edition of this work was compiled in 1834 by Jožef Kek, while the second and third editions from 1843 and 1854 were 'provided by Jan Šlaker', who already participated in the first edition. Žigon, Almasy and Lovšin attribute an important role in the 1854 third edition to Franc Močnik. Later editions were 'extensively reproduced and corrected' by Andrej Praprotnik. The 1898 edition, which was published after Praprotnik's death, was 'extensively multiplied and corrected by Jakob Dimnik' (Lesar, 2013 and Žigon and Almasy and Lovšin, 2017, 103-104).
