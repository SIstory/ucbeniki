NotesGiven that the work is listed in the list of approved textbooks for elementary schools (Volksschulen) as early as 1878, there are certainly earlier prints, but we have not been able to identify them so far. From 1889, the standard version coexisted and was published simultaneously with a more extensive revised edition, the latter was published at least in 1889. In 1896, a slightly shorter 'revised edition' was published. All variants were approved and simultaneously approved for use in school lessons.
