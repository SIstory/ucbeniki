NotesThe present work is Bakoš's reworking of Abecedarium szlowenszko, za drobno detzo, vőn szpűscheno from 1725. Kokolj and Horvat also write about the edition from 1793, which apparently has not been preserved to this day (Škafar, 1978, 15, 19 and Andoljšek, 1978, 61 and Kokolj and Horvat, 1977, 91 and Krajnc-Vrečko, 2013). The work was reprinted in 2017.
