NotesDespite the changes in the titles, it is the same textbook, and only a few texts have been added over the years. Andoljšek lists the editions from 1882, 1885, 1890, 1891, 1893, and 1902, but mentions that the literature also mentions the editions of 1887 and 1888. The problem is that in this case the counting of reprints printed in textbooks is incorrect (Andoljšek, 1976, 133 -134). The COBISS system also includes the 1888 edition, which is why it is still included in the list, as well as the unmentioned 1901 edition.
