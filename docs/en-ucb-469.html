NotesThe compiler of the present work was Andrej Zamejic. From 1889, the standard version coexisted and was published simultaneously with a substantially more comprehensive revised edition; the latter was published in 1889, 1890, 1893 and 1896. Both versions were approved and at the same time allowed for use in school classes. In the present entry, we combine both (Smolik, 2013).
